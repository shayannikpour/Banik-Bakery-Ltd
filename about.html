About Us

Who we are

Banik Bakery Ltd. is a Canadian importer and distributor supplying retailers and distributors nationwide. Despite the name, we specialize beyond baked goods, focusing on reliable sourcing and logistics for high-demand categories.

We currently handle coffee capsules, canned pineapple products, and seasonal fresh pomegranates.

How we work

  • Direct relationships with vetted manufacturers
  • Regulatory compliant labeling and documentation
  • Flexible MOQs, pallet & container programs
  • Responsive customer support and forecasting
